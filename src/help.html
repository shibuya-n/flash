Welcome to the flashcard app!

Studying topics

Select the topic you want to study by clicking on the dropdown under the menu, and then pressing go.

Click anywhere on the screen to flip the flashcard.

If you press "i got it wrong", then the card will be sent to the back of the deck. If you press "i got it right", you can move onto the next flashcard.

Press "back" on the top left of the screen anytime you want to quit the flashcards and return home.

What is challenge mode?

Challenge mode combines all the topics together. The cards will not be sent to the back of the deck if you get it wrong. You will be able to keep track of the cards you get right at the bottom of the screen
